Write call-to-action (CTA) sections for a public medical clinic.

Include:
1. Contact / Visit CTA:
   -> Encourage patients to visit the clinic during operating hours
   -> Suggest arriving early due to high patient volumes

2. Trust-Building CTA:
   -> Emphasise public healthcare values
   -> Highlight dignity, respect, and professional care
   -> Reassure patients about fair and ethical service

3. Location-Based CTA:
   -> Reference Rosslyn and Akasia
   -> Encourage local community members to use nearby healthcare services

Tone:
Supportive, respectful, and action-oriented.
No sales or booking pressure.
